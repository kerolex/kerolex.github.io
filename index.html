About

Alessio is a fourth-year Postgraduate Research student in Electronic Engineering and Computer Science at Queen Mary University of London, UK. He works on an Audio-Visual Intelligent Sensing Project with a joint studentship (2015-2019) of Queen Mary University of London and Fondazione Bruno Kessler (FBK, Italy) under the supervision of Prof. Andrea Cavallaro (QMUL) and Dr. Oswald Lanz (FBK).
