Alessio is a research assistant and Ph.D. candidate in Electronic Engineering at Queen Mary University of London (QMUL), UK. As research assistant, Alessio investigates vision and machine learning algorithms for performing human-to-robot handover under the CORSMAL project. During his Ph.D., Alessio investigated the problem of matching local image and spatio-temporal features between moving monocular cameras for applications like Collaborative Simultaneous Localisation and Mapping under the supervision of Prof. Andrea Cavallaro (QMUL) and Dr. Oswald Lanz (FBK). The Ph.D. was funded from 2015 to 2019 under the Audio-Visual Intelligent Sensing Project, a joint collaboration between QMUL and Fondazione Bruno Kessler, Italy.
